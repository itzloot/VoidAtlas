08 / BLACK & WHITE HOLES

Black Holes & White Holes

Black holes: Regions where gravity is so strong that nothing escapes. White holes: theoretical opposites.

Types: Stellar, Supermassive, Primordial
Event Horizon: Point of no return
Singularity: Infinite density
Time Dilation: Gravity slows time
White Holes: Not yet observed
DO YOU KNOW? Time near black holes moves slower than on Earth.
